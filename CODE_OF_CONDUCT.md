# 行为准则 / Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

作为LXCLUA项目的贡献者和维护者，我们承诺营造一个开放、热情、尊重和友好的社区环境，让所有参与者都能感受到归属感，无论其年龄、体型、残疾、种族、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何。

As contributors and maintainers of the LXCLUA project, we pledge to foster an open, warm, respectful, and friendly community environment where all participants feel a sense of belonging, regardless of their age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

### 积极行为示例 / Examples of Positive Behavior

- **尊重不同观点和经验**：尊重他人的意见和经验，即使与自己的不同
- **优雅地接受建设性批评**：以开放的心态接受反馈，并努力改进
- **关注社区的最佳利益**：考虑自己的行为对整个社区的影响
- **对其他社区成员表示同理心**：理解并支持其他社区成员
- **使用包容的语言**：使用尊重和包容的语言，避免使用冒犯性词汇
- **乐于助人**：愿意帮助其他社区成员，特别是新加入的成员

- **Respect differing viewpoints and experiences**: Respect others' opinions and experiences, even if they differ from your own
- **Gracefully accept constructive criticism**: Accept feedback with an open mind and strive to improve
- **Focus on what's best for the community**: Consider the impact of your actions on the entire community
- **Show empathy towards other community members**: Understand and support other community members
- **Use inclusive language**: Use respectful and inclusive language, avoid offensive terms
- **Be helpful**: Be willing to help other community members, especially new ones

### 不可接受的行为 / Unacceptable Behavior

- **使用冒犯性语言或图像**：使用基于性别、种族、宗教、性取向等的冒犯性语言或图像
- **骚扰行为**：包括但不限于骚扰性评论、冒犯性笑话、人身攻击等
- **发布他人的私人信息**：未经明确许可，发布他人的私人信息
- **恶意攻击**：故意破坏、贬低或攻击其他社区成员
- **其他不专业或不适当的行为**：不符合专业和尊重标准的行为

- **Use of offensive language or imagery**: Use of language or imagery based on gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, etc.
- **Harassment**: Including but not limited to harassing comments, offensive jokes, personal attacks, etc.
- **Publishing others' private information**: Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- **Malicious attacks**: Deliberately undermining, demeaning, or attacking other community members
- **Other unprofessional or inappropriate behavior**: Behavior that does not meet professional and respectful standards

## 责任与执行 / Responsibility and Enforcement

### 项目维护者的责任 / Responsibilities of Project Maintainers

项目维护者负责明确和执行我们的行为标准，并将对任何被视为不适当、威胁性、冒犯性或有害的行为采取适当和公平的纠正措施。

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our behavior standards and will take appropriate and fair corrective actions for any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### 执行流程 / Enforcement Process

1. **报告问题**：如果您遇到或目睹了违反行为准则的行为，请通过邮件联系项目维护者：contact@difierline.com
2. **调查**：项目维护者将对报告的问题进行调查，确保公平和客观
3. **采取行动**：根据调查结果，项目维护者可能会采取以下行动：
   - 私下警告违规者
   - 公开警告违规者
   - 暂时禁止参与社区活动
   - 永久禁止参与社区活动
   - 其他适当的措施
4. **通知结果**：项目维护者将向报告者和违规者通知处理结果

1. **Report the issue**: If you encounter or witness behavior that violates the code of conduct, please contact the project maintainers via email: contact@difierline.com
2. **Investigation**: Project maintainers will investigate the reported issue, ensuring fairness and objectivity
3. **Take action**: Based on the investigation results, project maintainers may take the following actions:
   - Privately warn the violator
   - Publicly warn the violator
   - Temporarily ban from community activities
   - Permanently ban from community activities
   - Other appropriate measures
4. **Notify results**: Project maintainers will notify the reporter and violator of the handling results

### 执行原则 / Enforcement Principles

- **尊重隐私**：保护报告者的隐私，除非获得明确许可
- **公平公正**：对所有社区成员一视同仁，不偏不倚
- **及时处理**：及时处理报告的问题，避免问题扩大
- **透明公开**：处理过程应该透明，结果应该公开（除非涉及隐私问题）

- **Respect privacy**: Protect the privacy of reporters unless explicit permission is obtained
- **Fair and impartial**: Treat all community members equally, without bias
- **Timely handling**: Handle reported issues promptly to avoid escalation
- **Transparency**: The handling process should be transparent, and results should be public (unless privacy issues are involved)

本行为准则适用于所有LXCLUA项目的社区空间，包括但不限于：

- GitHub仓库（问题、Pull Requests、评论等）
- 邮件列表

This code of conduct applies to all community spaces of the LXCLUA project, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, comments, etc.)
- Mailing lists
- Community discussion groups
- Offline events
- Other project-related communication channels

## 其他事项 / Other Matters

### 如何回应违规行为 / How to Respond to Violations

如果您看到有人违反行为准则：

- **公开场合**：请礼貌地提醒他们注意自己的行为，引导他们遵循行为准则
- **严重违规**：如果是严重的违规行为，请直接联系项目维护者，不要在公开场合与违规者争论

If you see someone violating the code of conduct:

- **Public场合**: Please politely remind them to be mindful of their behavior and guide them to follow the code of conduct
- **Serious violations**: If it's a serious violation, please contact the project maintainers directly, do not argue with the violator in public

### 如何寻求支持 / How to Seek Support

如果您因为他人的不当行为而感到不适，或需要支持，请联系项目维护者：contact@difierline.com

If you feel uncomfortable due to others' inappropriate behavior or need support, please contact the project maintainers: contact@difierline.com

### 修订和更新 / Revision and Update

本行为准则可能会根据社区的反馈和需求进行修订和更新。任何重大变更都将在GitHub仓库中公开讨论，并在实施前获得社区的共识。

This code of conduct may be revised and updated based on community feedback and needs. Any major changes will be publicly discussed in the GitHub repository and will require community consensus before implementation.
